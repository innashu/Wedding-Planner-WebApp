# Phase 7: Timeline View with Month-by-Month Wedding Planning Tasks

## Overview

Add a Timeline tab that displays a month-by-month view of wedding planning tasks from the current month (February 2026) through the wedding month (November 2026). Tasks are organized by category and based on comprehensive research from well-known wedding planning websites including The Knot, Zola, WeddingWire, and Joy.

**Wedding Details:**
- Date: November 8, 2026
- Location: Half Moon Bay, CA (Bay Area)
- Guest Count: ~150
- Ceremony: Jewish (honoring traditions)

Tasks compiled from:
- [The Knot - 12 Month Wedding Planning Countdown](https://www.theknot.com/content/12-month-wedding-planning-countdown)

### Post-Honeymoon: Name Change Tasks

**Step 1: Social Security Administration (Do First)**
- Visit local SSA office or mail Form SS-5
- Bring: certified marriage certificate + photo ID
- Free, ~2 weeks for new card
- Wait 48 hours after processing before DMV

**Step 2: California DMV (Do Second)**
- Schedule appointment at DMV.ca.gov
- Bring: new SS card (or receipt), current license, marriage certificate, proof of address
- Update voter registration and vehicle title while there

**Step 3: Passport**
- Submit Form DS-82 (renewal) or DS-11 (new)
- Include certified marriage certificate and photo

**Step 4: Financial & Other**
- Bank accounts and credit cards
- Employer/HR and payroll
- Health, car, and home insurance
- Utilities and subscriptions
- Professional licenses

## Edge Cases

- **Date boundary:** Use PST timezone consistently (Pacific Standard Time / America/Los_Angeles)
- **Month transitions:** Page should update when month changes (user can refresh)
- **Wedding month:** Special styling and content for November 2026
- **Collapsed state:** Months remember their expanded/collapsed state during session
- **Mobile:** Cards should be full-width and easily tappable to expand
- **Empty task:** Don't allow saving empty task text
- **First load seeding:** Only seed if timelineTasks collection is completely empty
- **Edit cancel:** Pressing Escape while editing reverts to original text
- **Delete all tasks:** Category still shows with just "+ Add task" button
- **Long task text:** Text should wrap, not overflow
